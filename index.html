Preparation

What is the Vratam

  • A simple, powerful puja to Lord Sri Venkateswara — open to anyone, any faith.
  • Two ways: the detailed method (this course) and a simple 20-minute method (read the 5 stories). Equal fruit to both.
  • The promise: performed with faith, the Lord Himself comes — as a guest, an animal, a bird — to take the prasad.
  • No fasting, no rigid rules. Purity of mind is what matters.
Talk-track — The Sri Venkateswara Vratam is one of the easiest and most beloved ways to seek the grace of Lord Srinivasa. It asks for nothing but faith and a pure mind. You may perform it in the morning or evening, at home, in a temple, or on a riverbank. In this course we walk the detailed method step by step.

Preparation

Setup — Mandapa, Kalasam, Materials

  • Clean the puja area thoroughly.
  • Set up a mandapam; place the photo of Kalyana Venkateswara with Sridevi & Bhudevi (or the Lord alone / a small idol / a one-rupee coin).
  • Arrange a Kalasam (water pot).
  • Keep ready: tulasi, coconut, fruits, flowers, jaggery, panchamrutham (milk, curd, ghee, honey, sugar), haldi & kumkum, akshata, incense, lamp.
  • Invite family & friends — all who attend and take prasad share the blessing.
Talk-track — A clean place invites a clean mind. Place the Lord's picture — ideally Kalyana Venkateswara with His consorts. If you don't have it, His image alone, a small idol, or even a coin to stand for Him is enough; the Lord looks at the heart. Place the Kalasam to the right. The Lord is especially fond of tulasi, so be sure to keep it.
